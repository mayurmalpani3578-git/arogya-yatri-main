About Arogya Yatri

A traveler on a path to healing — connecting patients worldwide with India's finest healthcare, culture, and wellness traditions.

Meaning Behind Arogya Yatri

'Arogya' means well-being, and 'Yatri' means traveler, both derived from Sanskrit. Together, Arogya Yatri means a traveler on a path to healing.

Our Story

Your Trusted Partner in Global Medical Care

Arogya Yatri is a dedicated medical tourism service by Ewan Business Solutions, created to help international patients access world-class healthcare in India with ease and confidence.

We bridge the gap between patients and India's top hospitals, experienced doctors, and affordable treatment options. Our goal is to ensure that every patient receives quality care while also experiencing India's warmth, culture, and hospitality.

With a strong focus on transparency, comfort, and personalized guidance, Arogya Yatri manages the entire journey from your first inquiry to your return home after treatment.

Our Promise

But our care doesn't stop at the hospital. We guide you through India's rich culture, ancient healing practices, and serene landscapes helping you heal physically, emotionally, and spiritually.

What We Do

Connecting you with the right doctors and hospitals based on your medical needs and preferences.

Travel Planning
