Simple & Transparent

How Arogya Yatri Works

A carefully designed journey that supports you from first consultation to recovery and long-term follow-up care.

Step 1: Share Your Medical Need

Fill out our online form or WhatsApp us your reports, and our care team connects with you within 24 hours.

Step 2: Get Expert Opinions & Cost Estimates

We connect with trusted Indian hospitals and specialists to get you personalized treatment plans and transparent pricing.

Step 3: Visa, Flights & Accommodation - All Arranged

We help you apply for your medical visa, plan travel, and arrange safe accommodation with interpreter support from Ewan.

Step 4: Warm Welcome & Hassle-Free Admission

Our team receives you at the airport, assists with check-in, and ensures smooth coordination during your treatment.

Step 5: Heal, Relax & Discover India

After treatment, enjoy a recovery stay, Ayurveda therapies, or local sightseeing - blending care with culture.

Step 6: Follow-Up and Continued Care

We stay in touch even after you return home, sharing medical reports, arranging follow-ups, and sending essential medicines to support your recovery.
